## Getting Started

### How to Log In

You can log in two ways:

**Option 1 — PIN Login (fast, for staff)**
1. Go to https://pos.karetechsolutions.com
2. Enter your 4–8 digit PIN
3. Tap **Login**

**Option 2 — Email Login (for managers and admin)**
1. On the login screen, click **Sign in with Email**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

> If you forget your PIN, ask the manager or admin to reset it in Settings → Users.

## The Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the **Dashboard**. From here you can:

- See today's sales summary (total sales, order count, top items)
- Navigate to any section using the sidebar on the left
- Quick-start cards link you to the most common tasks

## POS Terminal (Taking Orders)

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → POS Terminal

### Starting a New Order

1. Click **New Order** (or it opens automatically)
2. Select the **Register** if prompted (Meals POS, Pastry POS, etc.)
3. Browse products by tapping a category tab at the top

### Adding Items

- Tap any product to add it to the cart on the right
- Tap again to add another, or use the **+/-** buttons in the cart
- Products with modifiers will show a popup — select your options (spice level, add-ons, etc.)

### Applying a Discount

1. In the cart, tap **Discount**
2. Choose a preset discount or enter a custom percentage/amount
3. The total updates automatically

### Split Bill

1. Tap **Split** in the cart
2. Enter how many ways to split
3. Each portion shows separately for payment

### Processing Payment

1. Review the cart total
2. Tap **Charge**
3. Choose payment method:
   - **Cash** — enter amount tendered, the system calculates change
   - **Card** — confirm amount, process on your card machine
   - **Gift Card** — enter gift card number
4. Tap **Complete Order**

The order is saved and a receipt can be printed or sent.

## Viewing Orders

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Orders

- See all orders with their status (Completed, Open, Voided, Refunded)
- Use the **search bar** to find an order by number or customer name
- Use **date filters** to view orders from a specific day/range
- Click any order to see full details including line items and payment

### Voiding an Order

1. Open the order
2. Click **Void Order**
3. Confirm the action
4. The order is marked as voided and removed from sales totals

## Cash Control (Shifts)

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Cash Control

### Opening a Shift

1. Click **Open Shift**
2. Count your starting cash and enter the amount
3. Click **Open**

### Recording a Cash Drop or Payout

During your shift:
- **Cash Drop** — use when the till has too much cash; moves money to the safe
- **Payout** — use when paying someone from the till (petty cash)

Click the relevant button, enter the amount and a reason, then confirm.

### Closing a Shift

1. Click **Close Shift**
2. Count your ending cash and enter the amount
3. The system shows you the expected vs. actual cash
4. Confirm and close

## Inventory

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Inventory

### Viewing Stock Levels

- See all inventory items with current stock levels
- Items below their minimum level show in **red** (stock alert)
- Filter by category or search by name

### Recording Stock Movement

1. Click **Add Movement**
2. Choose movement type:
   - **Received** — new stock arrived
   - **Used** — consumed in production
   - **Wasted** — spoiled or discarded
   - **Adjustment** — manual correction
3. Enter the quantity and reason
4. Click **Save**

### Purchase Orders

1. Click **Purchase Orders**
2. Click **New Order**
3. Select supplier and add items with quantities
4. Save as **Draft** or mark as **Ordered**
5. When stock arrives, click **Receive** to update inventory

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: A customer wants a refund. How do I process it?**
Go to Orders → find the order → click **Refund**. Enter the amount to refund and confirm. The refund is recorded against the original payment method.

**Q: The till cash doesn't match. What do I do?**
When closing the shift, enter the actual cash count. The system records the difference as an overage or shortage. Report discrepancies to the manager.

**Q: How do I add a new staff member?**
Go to Settings → Users → New User. Set their name, email, role, and PIN. They can log in immediately.

**Q: A product is out of stock. How do I hide it from the POS?**
Go to Settings → Products → find the product → toggle **Active** to off. It will be hidden from the POS terminal.

**Q: How do I see yesterday's sales?**
Go to Reports → Sales Report. Use the date picker at the top to select yesterday's date.

**Q: The system is running slow. What should I do?**
Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). If the problem persists, contact Kareem at KareTech Solutions.
