## Logging In

Go to **[pos.karetechsolutions.com](https://pos.karetechsolutions.com)** in any web browser.

### PIN Login (Quick — for cashiers & kitchen staff)

1. On the login screen, enter your 4–8 digit PIN
2. Tap **Login**

> If your PIN doesn't work, ask the manager to check it in **Settings → Users**.

### Email Login (For managers and administrators)

1. Click **Sign in with Email** on the login screen
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Logging Out

Click your name at the bottom of the left sidebar, then click **Sign Out**.

## Dashboard (Home)

After logging in, you see the **Dashboard** — your daily overview.

**What you see depends on your role:**

- **Executive/Admin** — Today's revenue, order count, hourly sales chart, top sellers, payment breakdown, and quick-action cards
- **Cashier** — Quick link to open the POS, recent orders, and time clock
- **Kitchen/Checkoff** — Links to Kitchen Display and Production, active ticket count

**Sidebar Navigation:**
Use the left sidebar to move between sections. Navigation is organized into groups:

| Group | What's inside |
|-------|---------------|
| **Operations** | New Sale, Orders, Kitchen Display, Production, Time Clock |
| **Inventory** | Stock, Stock Alerts, Waste & Shrinkage, Variance Report, Suppliers, Labels |
| **Finance** | Cash Control, Expenses, Discounts, P&L, Sales Journal, Reconciliation, Currency Rates |
| **Insights** | Reports, Analytics, EOD Report, Labor Cost, Profitability |
| **Customers** | Customers, Loyalty Program, Gift Cards |
| **Sales** | Quotations, Invoices |
| **System** | Settings, Locations, Webhooks, Notifications, Audit Log |

> The sidebar only shows groups and sections your role can access. Cashiers see far fewer options than managers.

### Quick Navigation — Ctrl+K Command Palette

Press **Ctrl+K** (or **Cmd+K** on Mac) anywhere in the dashboard to open the **command palette** — a fast search that jumps to any page instantly.

1. Press **Ctrl+K** — palette opens with a search box
2. Type any page name (e.g., "inventory", "cash", "kitchen")
3. Arrow keys or click to highlight → **Enter** to navigate

The palette only shows pages your role can access, same as the sidebar.

## Staff Roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Cashier** | New Sale, Orders, Time Clock |
| **Kitchen / Checkoff** | Kitchen Display, Production, Time Clock |
| **Warehouse Clerk** | Inventory, Stock Alerts, Purchase Orders, Suppliers, Labels |
| **Accountant** | Reports, Analytics, Expenses, P&L, Labor Cost, Reconciliation |
| **Admin / Manager** | All of the above + Settings, Cash Control, user management |
| **Executive / Owner** | Full access — all pages and financial data |

### Default Landing Page

Each user can set their preferred startup page in **Settings → My Workspace**. The default is the Dashboard, but cashiers may prefer to start directly on New Sale, and kitchen staff on the Kitchen Display.

### PIN Security

After **5 failed PIN attempts** from the same device, the system locks out that IP address for 60 seconds. This is automatic and cannot be bypassed. If a legitimate user is locked out, they should wait 60 seconds or use email login instead.

## Sync & Offline Status

A small indicator at the top of the sidebar shows connection status:
- **Green dot** — connected and synced
- **Yellow dot** — syncing in progress
- **Red dot** — connection lost (offline mode)

When offline, the system continues operating with locally cached data. Orders placed offline are synced automatically when the connection is restored.
