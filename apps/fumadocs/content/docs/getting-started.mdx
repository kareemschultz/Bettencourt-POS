## Logging In

### PIN Login (All Staff)

1. Open **pos.karetechsolutions.com** in your browser (or launch the desktop app)
2. Enter your **PIN** (4–8 digits assigned by your manager)
3. Click **Log In**

<Callout type="warn">
After **5 failed PIN attempts**, your IP is locked out for 60 seconds. Wait and try again, or use email login if you are a manager.
</Callout>

### Email Login (Managers & Administrators)

1. Click **Sign in with Email**
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **Log In**

---

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you land on your default start page (configurable in Settings → My Workspace).

### Sidebar Navigation

Organised into groups: Operations, Inventory, Finance, Insights, Customers, Sales, System. The sidebar only shows sections your role has access to.

### Command Palette (Ctrl+K)

Press **Ctrl+K** to open a quick search. Type any page name to jump there instantly.

---

## Staff Roles

| Role | Access | Default Start Page |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| **Cashier** | POS, orders, time clock | New Sale |
| **Kitchen / Checkoff** | KDS and production | Kitchen Display |
| **Warehouse** | Inventory, purchase orders | Inventory |
| **Accountant** | Reports, expenses, invoices | Finance Dashboard |
| **Admin / Manager** | All above + settings, products, users | Dashboard |
| **Executive / Owner** | Full unrestricted access | Dashboard |
| **Custom Roles** | Tailored permissions | As configured |

## Sync Status Indicator

- **Green** — fully connected
- **Yellow** — connection intermittent
- **Red** — offline (desktop app only)
