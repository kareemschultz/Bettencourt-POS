---
title: Expenses
description: Track daily business expenses by supplier, filter by clicking summary cards, and export to CSV
---

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Expenses

Track business expenses (non-COGS) such as utilities, rent, staff meals, repairs, etc.

---

## Summary Cards (Quick Filter)

At the top of the page are **clickable summary cards** showing your expense totals for the selected period:

- **All Expenses** card — shows the grand total. Click it to reset to viewing all entries.
- **Supplier cards** (up to 3) — show the total spent with each top supplier.

**Click any supplier card to instantly filter the expense table to show only that supplier's entries** — full date, category, description, and amount for every transaction.

The active card is highlighted with a ring so you always know which filter is in effect.

---

## Filtering Expenses

Use the controls at the top to narrow down what you see:
- **From / To** date fields — view expenses for a specific period
- **Supplier** dropdown — filter by a specific supplier
- Clicking a summary card (above) is a quick-filter shortcut that also updates the dropdown

---

## Adding an Expense

1. Click **Add Expense** (top right)
2. Enter the **amount** (GYD)
3. Select the **supplier** (optional)
4. Select the **category** (Utilities, Rent, Supplies, etc.)
5. Enter a **description** of what the expense was for
6. Click **Save Expense**

---

## Editing or Deleting an Expense

Each row in the expense table has:
- **Pencil icon** — click to open the edit dialog and correct any field
- **Trash icon** — click to delete; a confirmation prompt appears before deletion

---

## Exporting

Click **Export** (top right) to download the current filtered expenses as a CSV file — useful for sharing with your accountant.

---

## Expense Table

The table shows every entry for the selected period with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Date | Date and time the expense was recorded |
| Supplier | Supplier name (if linked), shown as a colour-coded badge |
| Category | Expense category (Utilities, Rent, Supplies, etc.) |
| Description | What the expense was for |
| Amount | Expense amount in GYD |
| Authorized By | The manager who authorized it |

---

> Expenses feed into the **P&L Statement** automatically under "Operating Expenses".
