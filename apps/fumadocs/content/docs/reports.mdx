**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Reports

## Report Types at a Glance

| # | Report | What It Shows |
|---|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Summary | Revenue, orders, average order, payment breakdown |
| 2 | Sales by Day | Daily revenue trend with bar chart |
| 3 | Department Totals | Revenue split by department/category |
| 4 | Product Sales | Per-product units sold, revenue, ranking |
| 5 | Cashier Activity | Sales per cashier, order count, average |
| 6 | Sales by Payment | Cash vs card vs gift card breakdown |
| 7 | Hourly Sales | Revenue by hour of day (heatmap) |
| 8 | Z-Report | Formal end-of-shift financial summary |
| 9 | Voids | All voided orders with reasons and authorizers |
| 10 | Production | Opening stock, produced, sold, closing, variance |
| 11 | Weekly Trend | Week-over-week comparison chart |
| 12 | Tips | Tips by employee and payment method |
| 13 | Customer Analytics | New vs returning, spend per customer, LTV |

All reports support date range filtering and CSV export.

---

## Sales Report

Total revenue, order count, average order value, breakdown by payment method, sales by department/category, top products. Date presets: Today, Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, Custom. CSV export available.

---

## Analytics

Revenue trend (30/60/90 days), hourly sales patterns, day-of-week performance, ABC product analysis, customer insights, labor cost ratio.

---

## Sales Journal

Line-by-line transaction record: date, order number, items, gross, discounts, taxes, net, payment method. For accounting and bank reconciliation.

---

## Reconciliation

Compare expected vs actual cash and card totals for any day. Enter actual counts; differences flagged for review.

---

## Profitability

Profit margins by product and department: revenue, estimated cost, gross profit/margin %, food cost %. ABC classification. CSV export.

---

## P&L Statement

Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Operating Expenses, Labor, Net Profit/Loss.

---

## Labor Cost

Staff hours per day/week, hourly rates, total wages, labor cost as % of revenue.

---

## End of Day (EOD) Report

Daily closing summary: total sales, payment method breakdown, cash drawer summary, top items, shift summary, department totals. Print for any past date.

---

## Variance Report

Expected stock vs actual stock from physical counts. Green = within tolerance, Red = significant variance. Click item for movement history.

---

## Tips Report

Breakdown of all tips collected:
- **Summary**: total tips for the period
- **By Employee**: which staff members received tips and totals
- **By Payment Method**: tips on cash orders vs card orders

---

## Customer Analytics

New vs returning ratio, average spend per customer, lifetime value, top customers by spend.

---

## Currency Rates

Set exchange rates for USD, CAD, and other foreign currencies.

---

## Export & Download

Most reports support CSV export and print-ready PDF format.
