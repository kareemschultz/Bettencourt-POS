## Customers

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Customers

### Adding a Customer

1. Click **New Customer**
2. Enter: Name (required), Phone (required), Email (optional), Date of Birth (optional), Address (optional)
3. Click **Save**

### Customer Profile

Click a customer to view: contact details, order history, loyalty points balance and tier, gift card balances, price list assignment, and all quotations/invoices linked to this customer.

### Customer Statements

Generate a financial statement: open the customer profile → Click **View Statement** → select date range → the statement shows invoices, payments, and credit notes with a running balance. Click **Print Statement** for PDF.

### Customer Payment Ledger

Complete payment history across all invoices, including unapplied payments and allocations.

## Agencies / Organizations

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Agencies

Agencies represent businesses that place orders. Create agencies with name, contact person, position, phone, and address. Agencies can be selected when creating quotations and invoices.

---

## Loyalty Program

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Loyalty Program

Customers earn points on orders. Tiers: Bronze (1x), Silver (1.5x at 500+ points), Gold (2x at 1,000+ points). Redeem points at checkout.

---

## Discounts

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Customers → Discounts

Create named discount presets (e.g., "Staff Discount 10%") with type (Percentage/Fixed), value, and optional minimum order threshold. Appear as quick-select buttons at POS.

---

## Gift Cards

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Customers → Gift Cards

Issue cards with unique code and starting balance. Redeem via Gift Card payment method at checkout. View transaction history per card.

---

## Price Lists

Assign customer-specific pricing for wholesale, VIP, or staff purchases. See [Price Lists & Dynamic Pricing](/docs/pricelists).
