### How do I process a refund?
Go to **Orders** → find the completed order → click **Refund**. Enter the refund amount and a reason.

### What do I do about a till variance?
At shift close, enter the actual cash count. The system compares Expected vs Actual and records the variance. Report significant variances to your manager.

### How do I void a paid order?
Go to **Orders** → find the order → click **Void Order**. Only Admins and Executives can void. Enter a reason.

### How do I hide a sold-out product?
Go to **Settings → Products** → toggle **Active** off.

### How do I add a new staff member?
Go to **Settings → Users → New User**. Enter name, email, role, and PIN.

### How do I reset a staff member's PIN?
Go to **Settings → Users** → click the user → change the PIN field → Save.

### How do I view past sales?
Go to **Reports → Sales Report** → use the date picker to select any past period.

### How do I print the End of Day summary?
Go to **Reports → End of Day** → select the date → click **Print**.

### How can I find which cashier handled an order?
Go to **Orders** → open the order → the "Served by" field shows the cashier.

### How do I check a gift card balance?
Go to **Gift Cards** → search by the gift card code.

### Why isn't a customer earning loyalty points?
Ensure the customer was linked to the order before checkout. Points only accrue on completed orders.

### How do I change a product price?
Go to **Settings → Products** → click the product → update the price → Save. Takes effect immediately.

### How can I see who opened the cash drawer?
Go to **Cash Control → Shift History** for shifts. Check the **Audit Log** for no-sale drawer openings.

### How do I see expenses by supplier?
Go to **Expenses** → click a supplier card to filter, or click a vendor badge to open the Vendor Detail page.

### The POS is not loading — what should I do?
1. Press F5 to refresh. 2. Check internet. 3. Try Chrome. 4. Contact KareTech Solutions.

### Can I use the system on a tablet?
Yes. Works on screens 10 inches and larger. Desktop app recommended for dedicated terminals.

### What is Ctrl+K?
Opens the **Command Palette** — quick search to jump to any page.

### I entered my PIN wrong too many times
Wait 60 seconds (5-attempt lockout). Or use email login (managers only).

### An SMS notification shows "Failed"
Go to **Notifications → Log** tab. Common causes: wrong phone format (needs +592), daily limit reached, or carrier blocking.

### How do I connect to accounting software?
Go to **System → Webhooks** → add an endpoint. Contact KareTech for custom integration.

### How do I set my default startup page?
Go to **Settings → My Workspace** → select your preferred landing page.

### How do I display the menu on a TV?
Navigate to **pos.karetechsolutions.com/menu-board** → click fullscreen.

### How do I share the online ordering link?
Your link: **pos.karetechsolutions.com/order**. Share via WhatsApp or print as QR code.

## New Features FAQ

### How do I create a recurring invoice?
Go to **Finance → Recurring Templates → New Template**. Set type, customer, items, frequency, and next run date.

### How do I assign a price list to a customer?
Go to **Customers** → open the profile → select a price list → Save. See [Price Lists](/docs/pricelists).

### What are combo products?
Combo products bundle items at a package price. Create in **Settings → Products** by adding components. See [Settings](/docs/settings).

### How do I set up receipt printers?
Go to **Settings → Printers → New Printer**. See [Printers](/docs/printers).

### Can I use the system offline?
Yes, with the **desktop app**. See [Desktop App](/docs/desktop-app).

### How do I generate a customer statement?
Go to **Finance → Customer Statements** → select customer → set date range → **Print Statement**.

### How do I back up my data?
Go to **System → Backup**. Automatic nightly + on-demand. See [Backup](/docs/backup).

### How do I manage table reservations?
Go to **Reservations → New Reservation**. Enter customer, date, time, party size, and table. See [Reservations](/docs/reservations).

### How do I set up the floor plan?
Go to **Settings → Floor Plans → New Floor**. Drag and drop tables onto the grid. See [Floor Plans](/docs/floor-plan).

### Where can I see all report types?
Go to **Reports**. There are 13 report types including Sales, Department Totals, Tips, Production, and Customer Analytics. See [Reports](/docs/reports).
