## Staff Roles at a Glance

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Cashier** | POS Terminal, Orders, Time Clock |
| **Kitchen / Checkoff** | Kitchen Display, Production, Time Clock |
| **Warehouse** | Inventory, Purchase Orders, Stock Alerts |
| **Accountant** | Reports, Expenses, P&L, Labor |
| **Admin / Manager** | Everything above + Settings, Cash Control |
| **Executive / Owner** | Full access — all pages and financial reports |

> The sidebar only shows sections your role can access. If you can't find a section, ask your manager.
