## Time Clock

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Time Clock

All staff clock in/out. Managers can edit times for corrections and view total hours per employee. Feeds into Labor Cost report.

---

## Audit Log

**Navigate to:** Dashboard → Audit Log

Complete, immutable history of all system changes: logins, orders, product/price changes, settings, user accounts, cash events, invoice/quotation changes, payments. Filter by user, action type, and date range.

---

## Menu Schedules

**Navigate to:** Settings → Menu Schedules

Control which products are available at different times. Create schedules with name, active days, time range, and selected products/categories.

## Shift Handoffs

When a manager hands over a register mid-day without closing the cash session:

1. Go to **Cash Control**
2. Click **Initiate Handoff** on the active session
3. The incoming manager reviews the current float and counts
4. Click **Accept Handoff** to take ownership
5. The handoff is logged in the Audit Log with both users and the timestamp

This avoids closing and re-opening the cash session just to change the responsible person.
